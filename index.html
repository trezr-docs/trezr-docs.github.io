About Trezor.io/Start

Trezor is a leading hardware wallet designed to secure your cryptocurrencies. Trezor.io/Start (Version 22.9.3) provides you with everything you need to set up and use your Trezor device safely and efficiently.

How to Set Up Your Trezor

Setting up your Trezor is simple and straightforward. Follow our step-by-step guide to ensure your wallet is secure.

  1. Unbox your Trezor device.
  2. Connect it to your computer using the provided USB cable.
  3. Visit Trezor.io/Start (Version 22.9.3) to download the Trezor Bridge software.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the setup.
